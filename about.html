Our story

India does business

WaChat is a product of Serves Technology — a small team in Bengaluru building tools that help Indian SMBs communicate better with their customers.

Why we built WaChat

WhatsApp is where Indian customers are. The tools weren't.

Over 500 million Indians use WhatsApp daily. For millions of SMBs, it's the primary way customers ask questions, check on orders, and get support.

But the tools to manage those conversations professionally — as a team, at scale, with proper templates and analytics — were either too expensive, built for Western markets, or required a developer to set up.

We built WaChat because we believe Indian businesses deserve a WhatsApp platform that's fast to set up, priced fairly in INR, and actually built around how Indian teams work.
