You can connect a number in about 10 minutes using Meta's embedded signup.

## Before you start

- A phone number **not** currently active on a personal WhatsApp account
- Admin access to your Meta Business account
- Your business name and website

## Steps

1. [Create your account](https://weflux.in/register) and open **Settings → Connect number**.
2. Click **Connect with Meta** and sign in with your Facebook account.
3. Select or create your Meta Business and WhatsApp Business account.
4. Enter the phone number and verify it via SMS or call.
5. Set your display name — this is what customers see.

Once Meta approves (usually minutes), your number is live and you can send your first template.

## Troubleshooting

- **Number already in use:** remove it from the WhatsApp app first, then retry.
- **Display name rejected:** avoid generic names; use your real brand name.
