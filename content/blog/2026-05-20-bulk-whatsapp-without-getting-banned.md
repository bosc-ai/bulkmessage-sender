If you've searched for a "WhatsApp bulk message sender", you've probably seen tools that promise unlimited messages from your personal number. They work — until your number is banned. Here's the safe way.

## Unofficial senders vs the official API

Unofficial tools automate the regular WhatsApp app, which violates WhatsApp's terms. Numbers get banned in waves with no recovery. The **official WhatsApp Business API** is built for volume, supports templates, and protects your number with a published quality system.

BulkMessageSender runs entirely on the official Cloud API.

## Rules that keep your number healthy

1. **Get opt-in.** Only message people who agreed to hear from you.
2. **Respect categories.** Use Marketing, Utility or Authentication templates correctly.
3. **Warm up new numbers.** Start small and grow volume over days, not minutes.
4. **Make opting out easy.** It protects your quality rating.

## What you get on the official API

- Delivery, read and click receipts
- Template management and approval status
- A quality rating you can monitor
