1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. WaChat uses cookies sparingly — only what's needed to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and measure how the site is performing.

2. Cookies we use

Strictly necessary

These cookies are required for the Services to function. They cannot be disabled.

  • wc_session — keeps you signed in (HTTP-only, Secure, SameSite=Lax)
  • wc_csrf — protects against cross-site request forgery
  • wc_workspace — remembers which workspace you last opened

Preferences

  • wc_theme — your light / dark mode preference
  • wc_lang — your interface language

Analytics (opt-in)

Loaded only after you accept analytics in the cookie banner. We use a self-hosted analytics tool that anonymises IP addresses and does not set cross-site tracking cookies.

3. Third-party cookies

We do not embed third-party advertising or tracking cookies. Customer support widgets and embedded videos may set their own cookies; we list them in our sub-processors section.

4. Managing cookies

You can clear cookies from your browser's settings at any time. Disabling strictly-necessary cookies will sign you out and may break parts of the application. Analytics cookies can be toggled off in your account settings.
