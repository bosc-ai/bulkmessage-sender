1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Serves Technologies ("BulkMessageSender", "we", "our", "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the BulkMessageSender platform, including the website at bulkmessagesender.com and the application at weflux.in (collectively, the "Services").

BulkMessageSender is an authorised WhatsApp Business Solution Provider operating on Meta's WhatsApp Business Cloud API. This policy applies to information about you as a customer of BulkMessageSender ("Customer") and to information about your end-customers that flows through our Services on your behalf ("End-User Data").

2. Information we collect

2.1 Customer account information

When you sign up for BulkMessageSender, we collect your name, work email address, company name, phone number, and billing information. This is information you provide to us directly.

2.2 End-User Data

When you send messages through BulkMessageSender, we process your end-customers' phone numbers, message content, message metadata (delivery and read status, timestamps), and any custom contact properties you configure. You are the data controller for End-User Data; we are a data processor acting on your instructions.

2.3 Usage and device information

We collect logs of your interactions with the Services (pages visited, API calls made, IP address, browser and OS metadata, timestamps) for security, debugging and product improvement.

2.4 Cookies

We use a small number of cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

3. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services
  • Deliver messages to and from your end-customers through Meta's WhatsApp Cloud API
  • Bill you and pass through Meta's conversation charges at cost
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to security incidents and policy violations
  • Communicate with you about your account, security alerts, and service updates
  • Comply with legal obligations under Indian and international law

We do not sell End-User Data to third parties. We do not use End-User Data to train machine-learning models. We do not share End-User Data with Meta beyond what is necessary to deliver your messages through the Cloud API.

4. When we share information

We share information only as follows:

  • Service providers and sub-processors: Cloud hosting, payment processors, error monitoring and customer support tools. A current list is at bulkmessagesender.com/legal/subprocessors.
  • Meta Platforms: Message payloads transit Meta's WhatsApp Cloud API to reach your end-customers. Meta's processing is governed by their own privacy policy.
  • Legal compliance: When required by valid legal process, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of BulkMessageSender, our customers, or the public.
  • Business transfers: If BulkMessageSender is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred subject to this Policy.

5. Data retention

We retain Customer account information for as long as your account is active and for a period of 30 days after closure, after which it is anonymised or deleted.

End-User Data (messages, contacts) is retained for the duration of your subscription and for 30 days thereafter, unless you delete it sooner or request immediate deletion. See Data Deletion for details.

6. Security

BulkMessageSender operates a SOC 2 Type II audited and ISO 27001 certified information security programme. Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Access to production systems requires two-factor authentication and is logged and audited. See our Security page for the full controls inventory.

7. Your rights

If you are an end-customer whose data is being processed by a BulkMessageSender Customer, please contact that Customer directly to exercise your rights. Where we act as the data controller (for our own Customer account information), you may:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Port your data to another service
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent

Contact privacy@serves.in to exercise any of these rights. We respond within 30 days.

8. International data transfers

BulkMessageSender hosts data primarily in Mumbai (AWS ap-south-1). Limited operational data (e.g. payment processing, error logs) may be processed in other jurisdictions through our sub-processors. Where data is transferred outside India, we rely on appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses.

9. Children

The Services are not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email to active Customer accounts at least 30 days before they take effect.
