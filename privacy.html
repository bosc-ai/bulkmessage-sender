Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 May 2026 · Serves Technology Pvt. Ltd.

1. Overview

WaChat is a WhatsApp Business Platform product built and operated by Serves Technology Pvt. Ltd. ("Serves," "we," "us," "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and delete information when businesses, administrators, and their customers interact with WaChat services.

By using WaChat you agree to this policy. If you are using WaChat on behalf of a business, you represent that you have authority to bind that business.

2. Information we collect

Account information

When you register for WaChat, we collect your name, email address, business name, phone number, role, and billing information.

WhatsApp Business data

Through Meta's Embedded Signup flow, we process WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) IDs, phone number IDs, business asset identifiers, message template metadata, webhook event payloads, message delivery status metadata, and access tokens required to operate the service.

Usage data

We collect information about how you use WaChat including pages visited, features used, conversation counts, and API call logs. This helps us improve the product and debug issues.

3. WhatsApp and Meta data

We use data received from Meta only to provide the WhatsApp Business Platform services you have requested. We do not sell WhatsApp user data or use it for unrelated advertising or profiling.

whatsapp_business_management: Used to read WABA and phone number identifiers after Embedded Signup, manage message templates on your behalf, and subscribe to webhook events. Data is stored only within your isolated workspace.

whatsapp_business_messaging: Used to send approved template messages to customers who have opted in to receive communications from your business. Message delivery metadata (status, timestamp) is retained for support and audit purposes only.

4. Message content

WaChat may process template content, message metadata, delivery status, and customer replies as needed to operate inbox and support workflows. You are responsible for obtaining valid opt-in consent from your customers and for complying with WhatsApp's Business Messaging Policy and applicable law.

5. How we use your information

  • To provide, operate, and improve WaChat services
  • To send you product updates, security notices, and billing communications
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • To generate aggregated, anonymised analytics

6. Sharing your information

We share information only with:

  • Service providers who help us host, secure, and operate WaChat (cloud hosting, payment processors, email providers). They are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. as required to provide the WhatsApp Business Platform integration.
  • Legal authorities when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users and the public.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

7. Data retention and deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When you close your account or request deletion, we delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

To request deletion of your data, visit our Data Deletion Instructions.

8. Data storage and security

Your data is stored on servers in Mumbai, India (primary) with failover in Singapore. Data in transit is protected by TLS 1.3. Data at rest is encrypted with AES-256. We maintain access controls, audit logging, and regular security reviews.

See our Security page for full details.

9. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. You may also object to certain processing or withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. To exercise these rights, email privacy@serves.in. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate the service and optional analytics cookies to understand usage. See our Cookie Policy for details.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email or via the WaChat dashboard at least 14 days before they take effect. Continued use of WaChat after changes constitutes acceptance.
