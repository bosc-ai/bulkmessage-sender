How it works

From authorization to active workspace in minutes.

1

Authorize your assets

Sign in to Facebook for Business, choose your business portfolio, select your WhatsApp Business Account, and share access. No IDs shared manually.

2

BulkMessageSender creates your workspace

BulkMessageSender stores your WABA ID and phone number ID, exchanges the authorization code for a system user token, and prepares your account for Cloud API operations.

3

Webhook monitoring activates

BulkMessageSender subscribes to message status, template update, and account change events. All callbacks are captured and visible to your team in the platform.
