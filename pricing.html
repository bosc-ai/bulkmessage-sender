About Meta conversation charges

What you pay Meta — at cost, no markup

WhatsApp charges per conversation (not per message). Rates vary by country and conversation type (marketing, utility, service, authentication). For India-to-India conversations, marketing rates are approximately ₹0.58/conversation and utility/service are free within 24h windows. WaChat passes these through at cost — we make nothing on Meta fees. See full Meta pricing table →
