Data Deletion Instructions

How to request deletion of your WaChat account and all associated data.

Your right to deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data and your organisation's data stored by WaChat. This includes account information, conversation history, contact records, template metadata, and any data collected during your use of the platform.

How to request deletion

Option 1 — Self-service (fastest)

Log in to WaChat at wachats.serves.in and go to Settings → Account → Delete account. This will immediately revoke all access and queue your data for deletion within 30 days.

Option 2 — Email request

Send an email to privacy@serves.in with subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your WaChat account
  • Your business name
  • A description of what data you want deleted (or "all data")

We will confirm receipt within 2 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.

Option 3 — WhatsApp

Message our support line at +91 888 440 6029 with your account email and the text: "I request deletion of my WaChat account and data."

What gets deleted

  • Your WaChat account and all agent accounts under your organisation
  • All conversation history and message metadata
  • Contact records, tags, and custom attributes
  • Template definitions and campaign history
  • Automation flows and bot configurations
  • API keys and webhook configurations
  • Billing and usage records (except where required for tax compliance)
  • OAuth tokens and WhatsApp Business Account linkages

What we retain (and why)

Some data may be retained beyond 30 days where required by law:

  • Financial records — Indian law requires retention of financial records for 7 years
  • Legal holds — If we are subject to a legal investigation relating to your account
  • Aggregated analytics — Anonymised, non-identifiable data that cannot be linked back to you

Facebook / Meta data

WaChat does not retain WhatsApp message content beyond what is needed for inbox operation. On account deletion, all WhatsApp-related tokens and business asset identifiers are revoked and purged. For data held directly by Meta (your WABA, phone number, templates), refer to Meta's own data deletion options.

Confirmation

Once deletion is complete (within 30 days of your request), we will send a confirmation email. If you no longer have access to your account email, please include an alternative contact in your deletion request.
