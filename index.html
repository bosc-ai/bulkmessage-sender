Built on the official Cloud API

No middleman. No shared numbers. No markup.

Your business connects directly to the official WhatsApp Business Cloud API. We don't resell numbers, we don't pool messaging volume, and we don't mark up conversation charges. You see what Meta charges, billed at cost.

Your own verified number

The phone number you use is registered to your business and verified directly with Meta. Not pooled, not shared, not rented.

Your own templates

Templates are submitted and approved under your business account. Edit, version, and version‑control them inside BulkMessageSender.

Direct, transparent billing

Conversation charges flow from Meta to you, at cost. BulkMessageSender earns from the platform fee only — never the message volume.
